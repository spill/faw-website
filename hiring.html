Full job description

We are a locally owned, family-run auto repair shop looking for an experienced, reliable Auto Mechanic to join our small team. We take pride in honest work, loyal customers, and maintaining a respectful, professional workplace.

The Automotive Technician will work collaboratively in a fast-paced environment, ensuring all vehicles are serviced to the highest standards.

Duties

  • Perform comprehensive automotive diagnostics to accurately identify issues and determine necessary repairs (strong diagnostic ability is a big plus).
  • Conduct routine vehicle maintenance, including oil changes, tire rotations, and brake inspections.
  • Repair and replace components of automotive systems such as transmissions, powertrains, suspension, steering, and electrical systems.
  • Utilize hand tools and power tools effectively to complete repairs efficiently and safely.
  • Interpret schematics and technical manuals to troubleshoot complex mechanical and electrical problems.
  • Maintain accurate records of services performed and parts used for each vehicle.
  • Communicate clearly with the Service Writer/Owner regarding customer vehicle needs and repair recommendations.
  • Collaborate with team members to ensure timely completion of services while maintaining quality standards.

Requirements

  • Minimum 5+ years of proven experience as an Automotive Technician or Service Technician.
  • Must have your own tools.
  • Must have a valid driver’s license.
  • Strong mechanical knowledge with hands-on experience in automotive repair and maintenance.
  • Proficiency in automotive diagnostics is strongly preferred, including experience working with advanced systems such as ADAS (preferred).
  • Familiarity with a wide range of vehicle makes and models is a plus.
  • Diesel engine repair experience is advantageous.
  • Experience using hand tools and power tools safely and effectively.

What we offer

  • Fast-paced, family-style work environment
  • Steady workload
  • Competitive pay based on experience (starting at $30/hour and up based on experience)
  • Long-term opportunity with a stable, locally trusted shop

Additional details

  • Experience: Auto Mechanic/Technician: 5 years (Preferred)
  • License/Certification: CDL (Preferred)
  • Ability to Commute: Fremont, CA 94539 (Required)
  • Ability to Relocate: Fremont, CA 94539: Relocate before starting work (Preferred)
  • Work Location: In person
