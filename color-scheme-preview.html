Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

Do you offer warranties on repairs?

Yes! We stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties on both parts and labor. Specific warranty terms vary by service, and we'll explain all coverage details before any work begins.

What makes and models do you service?

We service all makes and models, including domestic, Asian, and European vehicles. Our ASE-certified technicians have the expertise and equipment to handle everything from routine maintenance to complex repairs.

Do you provide free estimates?

Absolutely! We offer free estimates for most repairs. We'll diagnose the issue, explain what needs to be fixed, and provide a detailed estimate before any work begins. No surprises.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), and debit cards for your convenience.

How long do most repairs take?

Repair times vary depending on the service needed and parts availability. We'll provide you with an estimated completion time when you drop off your vehicle and keep you updated throughout the process.

Do I need an appointment?

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure we can give your vehicle the attention it deserves. Call us at 510-490-8883 to schedule.
